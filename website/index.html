Mentor

Supporting Students

Do you sometimes feel out of your comfort zone with how you can become a better thinker and an effective learner?
So here we come up with MENTOR, a beautifully designed and user friendly platform which serves as a medium between learners and educators aiming to help users by supporting them to become effective self-managed learners.

Learn with ease.

“BRINGING LEARNING TO PEOPLE” We see MENTOR as a vehicle to make an impact on the educational ground of the society. We aim to provide education anywhere at anytime to everyone. It facilitates the provision of various educational resources on the fingertips widening its benefits by enabling users to share their ideas and innovations through blogging. What makes us outstand is the feature of MENTOR to provide resources in local languages such as Hindi and Gujarati for now, which we are looking forward to extend in future. The main focus of this project is to guide the users in all the possible ways and helping them in defining their career and interests..
